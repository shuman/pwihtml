Social Media

Like it or not, social media is here to stay. By using social media to connect with people where they are, you can generate support and interest in your organization from people in your own hometown, to people in Timbuktu. We have managed dozens of social media pages and run multiple campaigns for some of the largest multinational brands in the world.

Since so many people are on social media every day, it's crucial to determine what platforms your organization should be on. There's a vast array of options, including Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, Pinterest, Google+, and Vine, and we’ll help you determine which platforms are the best fit for furthering your mission and vision.

Advertising

Do you think online advetising is the way to go for your organization? We've been part of successful online advertising campaigns for some of the largest to some of the smallest brands in the country. We can help you by growing your email list, getting better open rates, improving click-through rates, and making the most of the list you currently have.

Remarketing

Many people engage with your organization to the point of donation or purchase, but disengage before the transaction is complete. We can help you connect with people who reached the checkout on your site and then dropped off, and remind them of their interactions with you.

Once we know someone was interested, we can target them with ads based on their interests. We can also work on “up-selling” them by suggesting that they donate to similar causes or buy products similar to the ones already in their cart.

A/B Testing

How do you know if your website is set up for success? One thing we’ve witnessed time and time again is the power of measuring data and A/B testing.

Our leadership team has great experience with A/B testing, and we’ll help you figure out what converts best on your website. It may be as simple as changing the call to action, the images, or the look of the buttons. We'll find the most effective ways for people to navigate through your website based on what we know converts well.

Regardless of how big or small the changes, there are always improvements that will make a big impact.

SEO

We live in the era of the search engine, and Google reigns supreme. A strong presence on Google is crucial to acquiring new supporters and getting your organization in front of the crowd. When someone searches for a keyword or phrase related to your work, where does your organization show up? Page 100 of 100,000+ results? We'll help you move up the ranks on Google so that when people are searching for topics or organizations related to your field, they’ll easily find you. You'll drive more traffic to your website, increase online visibility, and boost interest in your work with a strategic SEO plan.
