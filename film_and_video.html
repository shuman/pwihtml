Film/Video & Recording

Animated Videos:

A high quality animated video can explain an idea or inspire an audience in as little as 30 seconds. Our team will produce powerful, concise, and informative videos to expose and market your work, with a special focus on engaging your target audience and motivating them to action.

Documentary Films:

Sometimes words fall short, and people don't fully understand what you're doing until they can see it for themselves. Even your supporters won't always have the opportunity to see your work up close and in person. For this reason, our team travels across the country and across the world to capture the essence of your organization on film and present it in a memorable and compelling way.

Photography:

A picture is worth a thousand words. PWI can create stunning photographs highlighting your products, facilities, events, employees, or any anything else you need. Our team takes care of the props, lighting, editing, composition, and equipment to produce memorable images that speak volumes about your company.

Radio/TV Advertising:

From radio to TV advertisements, the PWI team can script, film, produce, and edit ads that memorably and succinctly convey what your organization is all about. We'll also make sure your ads end up in front of the right audience by distributing them to the media channels that are the best fit for your organization.

Video Editing:

Have some video footage you don't know what to do with? PWI can edit your existing video footage to produce a cohesive and effective message that shows your audience who you really are.
