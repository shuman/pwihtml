who They Are

Chipotle is a fast-casual national food chain specializing in gourmet burritos and tacos.

01 SEO

We optimized Chipotle’s website to show up first in healthy food searches online. Our team improved the Chipotle website to decrease the average page load time, enabling the site to handle more traffic. We also optimized Chipotle’s website for social media, allowing for better interaction between the company’s website and social media platforms.

02 Advertising

We designed and implemented a billboard campaign for Chipotle to generate more traffic to their restaurants.

03 App Design

We built an app that enabled customers to order menu items on their phone and have items waiting for them to pick up in a Chipotle store.
