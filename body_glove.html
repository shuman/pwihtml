who They Are

Body Glove Mobile is the third largest cell phone case distributor in the country, specializing in cases and accessories that are both durable and fashionable.

SEO

01

Body Glove Mobile’s website was not standing out in watch-related searches online. So our team created a plan to optimize the Body Glove Mobile site to consistently appear in online searches, with the result of increased traffic to the site.

02

Social Media

PWI revamped Body Glove Mobile’s social media platforms to help them cater more effectively to their target audience and increase their visibility online. PWI also gave Body Glove Mobile training on how to most effectively manage their social media accounts and sync their existing social acounts to allow for ease of posting .

03

Ecommerce Website

PWI created a brand new ecommerce website for Body Glove Mobile, enabling them to smoothly handle the increase in sales and web traffic from their improved social media and SEO. The Body Glove Mobile website features a high functioning aesthetically appealing online store where consumers browse and order products in a user-friendly format. By optimizing the Body Glove Mobile website, we significantly improved the company’s conversion rate and boosted their online sales.

We work with MailChimp and create custom HTML templates in-house.

03

Email Design

After the launch of the new Body Glove Mobile website, PWI created a unique email template announcement. Our email encouraged Body Glove Mobile's subscribers to explore the new site and shop at the online store, resulting in a boost of regular traffic and sales on the site.
