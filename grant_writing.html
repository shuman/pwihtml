Grant Writing

Project World Impact’s team is certified to apply for online advertising grants for nonprofits. This will significantly increase your chances of gaining funding for your nonprofit. We have obtained dozens of grants for nonprofits already, with a success rate of more than 95%. We're confident we can help you obtain a grant that will allow you to reach more people online.

The grant we are certified in applying for gives your organization $10,000 per month to use for online advertising. The grant lasts two years, supplying you with $240,000 total to put towards strategically marketing your organization online.

Grant Management

Whether Project World Impact secured an online advertising grant for your organization, or you secured a grant on your own, we can help you effectively manage the funds.

While you'll have full access to managing the grant money yourself, our team is experienced in managing the $10,000 per month to maximize its potential.

We offer the options of a one-time grant management consultation or long-term advising relationship, depending on the individual needs of your organization.
