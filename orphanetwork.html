who They Are

ORPHANetwork is a nonprofit working in Nicarauga to rescue and care for orphaned children and prevent at-risk children from being abandoned. Through partnerships with local churches, ORPHANetwork creates opportunities for children to thrive and succeed while meeting their physical, spiritual, nutritional, and educational needs.

01

Design

Project World Impact designed and created a one-of-a-kind poster for ORPHANetwork to gift their donors in celebratation of the organization's 15th anniversary.

PWI designed an informational handout about ORPHANetwork's year in review, distributed to donors in conjunction with the anniversary celebration poster.

02

Website

PWI designed and built a new website for ORPHANetwork and created a new online donation platform through the site.

03

Email Design

After the launch of the new ORPHANetwork website, PWI created a unique email template announcement alerting donors, staff, and volunteers about the new site.

We work with MailChimp and create custom HTML templates in-house.

04

Marketing

Project World Impact created and implemented several marketing strategies for ORPHANetwork, including a comprehensive PR initiative that increased local and international awareness about the organization's work.
