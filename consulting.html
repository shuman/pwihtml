Online

Online marketing is the fastest way to make or break any business. In a world where new URLs are hard to come by and SEO and social media are essential, it’s important to know how to set yourself apart.

At Project World Impact, we teach organizations how to establish and maintain a strong online presence. We invest time in staying on the cutting edge of the technology learning curve in order to forecast effective strategies to connect you to interested consumers.

Lean

Toyota Motor Company revolutionized automobile manufacturing by implementing “lean manufacturing” focused on continuous improvement, elimination of waste, and support for the operators in their plant. Even if you're a charity or nonprofit, these same principles can make a huge difference in your organization.

Our team has decades of experience in implementing lean principles around the world. By working with PWI, everyone in your organization will learn to use their skills, knowledge, and ability to drive improvement and make your organization better each day.
